- 每类80张图片

### 选项2：动物分类

## 🎨 界面升级

### 更好看的网页

```python
with gr.Blocks(theme=gr.themes.Soft()) as demo:
    gr.Markdown("# 🌸 智能花卉识别系统")
    
    with gr.Row():
        with gr.Column():
            input_image = gr.Image(type="pil")
            submit_btn = gr.Button("识别", size="lg")
        
        with gr.Column():
            output_label = gr.Label(num_top_classes=5)
            output_text = gr.Textbox(label="详细信息")
    
    submit_btn.click(predict, inputs=input_image, outputs=[output_label, output_text])

demo.launch()
```
